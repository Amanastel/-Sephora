March 25, 2022
Anaita Shroff Adajania is THE celebrity stylist and a leading tastemaker within India's fashion industry. Her immense contribution to style can be seen in the costumes of Bollywood classics over the years, like Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge Dhoom 2 Love Aaj Kal and 'Cocktail. The iconic fits of characters within these films have inspired a generation, but that isn't her only mark. She is also the personal stylist to the biggest names in Bollywood, including Katrina Kaif and Deepika Padukone, both of whom have received acclaim for their red-carpet looks and fashion choices. We were glad to sit down with Anaita and discuss how she merges fashion and beauty together, to create a look that is both seamless and stunning, and reflects the innate power of your beauty. While you can watch all her suggestions on our social media pages in their entirety, here are her recommendations for Making a Look.
How To Incorporate Colored Make-Up Into Your Routine
Product: Sephora Collection | Intense Ink Waterproof Liquid Eyeliner
Liquid Eyeliners can create those colorful, graphic looks that simply bring a touch of brightness, even if the rest of your wardrobe is muted. It?s just the right accent of color that can go with tropical prints or a casual style.

Why is this Anaitas Choice?
When you pair something like this with everyday casuals, it makes your whole look pop. I also absolutely love trying new graphic liners like these ones from Sephora Collection.
The Trendy Summer Make-up Bag Addition Product: Sephora Collection | Color Hit Nail Polish
Go bright with your beauty in the summers to truly claim the vibe of the season. Your color palette should be breezy and filled with a plethora of colors like yellow, peaches and corals. Bright and bold hues are everything this season.

Why are these Anaitas Choice?
I love a body shine starting with the clavicles, down to the toes! That, along with a bright nail color. White is my current favorite, but I also love Neon French Nail Tips. Fashion wise, I'd go with the micro mini skirt trend, bikinis with oversized shirts, big bold prints in dresses and colored enamel.
The One Beauty Rule To Follow, Irrespective Of Trends
Product: Sunday Riley | CEO Vitamin C Rich Hydration Cream
Moisturizing is essential, and a tip that Anaita learnt from her grandmother. She has a point too, as moisturizing daily keeps your skin healthy, plump and ready to take on the day

Why is this Anaitas Choice?
Keeping one's skin hydrated is so important, especially when the moisturizer is rich in Vitamin C.
The Signature Day to Night Beauty Routine Product: Natasha Denona | Glam Eyeshadow Palette & I Need A Nude Lipstick
Eyeshadow that glitters paired with a classy nude lipstick is a killer combination that brings both sparkle and suave to your look. The combination is built to complement each other while not looking overly done-up?.

Why is this Anaitas Choice?
I'll add a sparkly brown shadow to my smudge liner as it helps define the ends more. With that, I'll use a light, nude lipstick, and a tan bronzer on my face, clavicles and shoulders. You can really see how they complement each other!
One Beauty Product That Makes You Feel Empowered Product: Guerlain | Cils D'Enfer Mascara
A brown mascara for the day or a black one for the night, these are a great addition to a make-up kit, and a product that brings a big difference to your look with just minimal use.

Why is this Anaitas Choice?
In general a bold Mascara like this one does it for me. It just brings the boom.
One Beauty Fear You Have Recently Overcome Product: LANCOME | L-Absolu Rouge Drama Matte Lipstick
Wearing bold colors can be a risky proposition, but when accessorized in the right way with your outfit, they are just what you need to make an impression.

Why is this Anaitas Choice?
I had stopped wearing bold colors like this on my lips, but I recently discovered it at a shoot for Lancome where I tried one and now I'm completely hooked. Bold colors are back in the bag for me!
